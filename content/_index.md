I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Institute for Social Sciences at the Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin. My research spans political sociology, political behavior, party systems, and political economy, with a particular focus on generational and age-related differences in voting and attitudes in Western European societies.

Before joining the Humboldt-Universität, I was a researcher at the Institute of Political Science at the University of Zurich, where I completed my PhD in 2025 with a dissertation on generational voting effects in Western Europe. During my PhD, I was also a guest researcher at the University of Amsterdam.

My research has appeared in *World Politics*, *West European Politics*, *Electoral Studies*, the *European Journal of Political Research*, and the *Swiss Political Science Review*, among others.
